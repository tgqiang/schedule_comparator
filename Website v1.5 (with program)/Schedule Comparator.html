How do I use this comparator?


For this manual entry comparison, you enter the dates that you are available for stuff via the calendar date-picker interface in this website. Your friends or group members should also do the same and then the program would help you to find out the dates and times where all of you are available to meet up.

For the calendar interface, click on the desired date to select it. Click on the selected date again if you want to remove it from your selection
